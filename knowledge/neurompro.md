## Dosage / how to use
- Follow the dosage printed on the bottle label.
- Take consistently, daily, at the same time if possible.
- Take with water — some customers prefer with a meal to minimize any stomach discomfort.

## Expected timeline
- Not immediate.
- Recommended evaluation window: **180–210 days** of consistent use.
- Early signs (sharper focus, mental clarity in the morning) often show in the first 30–60 days. Memory-specific improvements are more gradual and usually observable after 90–120 days of consistency.
