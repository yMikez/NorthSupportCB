# Thermo Burn — product knowledge

## Product
- **Name:** Thermo Burn (sometimes labeled "Thermo Burn Pro")
- **Type:** Natural dietary supplement (capsules, bottle form).
- **Health focus:** Weight management and fat-loss support.
- **Website:** https://getthermoburn.com/front-cb

## Positioning
- Supports healthy weight management goals.
- Designed to complement diet and exercise — not a replacement.
- Natural, science-backed formula.

⚠️ Never promise specific weight loss numbers ("lose X pounds in Y days"). Use "supports", "helps", "many customers report".

## Package options
- 2-bottle bundle
- 3-bottle bundle
- 6-bottle bundle (best value, recommended for full protocol)

## Dosage / how to use
- Follow the dosage printed on the bottle label.
- Take daily, consistently.
- Works best combined with moderate caloric control and light-to-moderate physical activity — be honest about this when it comes up.

## Expected timeline
- Not immediate.
- Recommended evaluation window: **180–210 days**.
- Most customers notice energy/appetite changes in the first 15–30 days; measurable body composition changes typically emerge after 60–90 days of consistent use *plus* lifestyle effort.

## Retention hooks specific to Thermo Burn
- "Weight management is a slow curve — the first few weeks are about building the internal environment, not the visible result. You may be right at the tipping point."
- "Some customers notice better results by slightly increasing to **2 capsules per day** for 2–3 weeks. Want to try that before deciding?"
- Lifestyle is a huge factor here: ask gently about sleep, water intake, stress, and whether they've been able to stay consistent with meals.
- If the customer has a plateau: "Plateaus are really common around week 3–5 — they're actually a sign the body is recalibrating. Pushing through that wall is often where the biggest changes happen."

## Common questions
- **"I didn't lose any weight."** → Ask how long they've used it consistently and what their routine looks like. Often it's < 30 days of use, skipped days, or no diet/activity adjustment.
- **"Does it have caffeine / stimulants?"** → "Refer to the ingredient label on your bottle for exact composition. Can I help with something specific — like how you're feeling after taking it?" Do NOT invent ingredients.
- **"Can I take it if I have a thyroid condition / diabetes / high BP?"** → "Those are really important to check with your doctor before starting any supplement. Your safety is what matters most."
- **"Can I take it while intermittent fasting / keto?"** → "Many customers do. Just take it consistently at the same time each day that works for your schedule."
- **"I'm only losing 1 lb per week."** → Reframe as positive: "That's actually a very healthy, sustainable rate — people who lose faster tend to rebound."

## Delivery
- Processing: 1–2 business days.
- Domestic: 3–7 business days.
- International: longer — offer to check tracking.

## Refund & return
- 60-day money-back guarantee from shipping date.
- We handle returns internally — customer does NOT ship anything back.

## Cases to offer the refund immediately (besides anger)
- Adverse reaction (racing heart, severe insomnia, dizziness).
- Customer reveals a heart condition or is on BP medication — never try to retain, suggest stopping and talking to a doctor.
