- Follow the dosage printed on the bottle label.
- Take consistently, every day, at roughly the same time.

- Not immediate. Natural supplements build up in the system.
- Recommended evaluation window: **180–210 days** of consistent use.
- Many customers notice subtle changes in energy and morning response within 30–60 days; noticeable sexual function improvements typically emerge between day 60 and day 120.

## Retention hooks specific to Max Vitalize
Use these when a customer (not hostile) wants to quit:
- "Vitality protocols for men work on a 3–6 month window — you may be very close to the point where results become consistent."
